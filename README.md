# Artificial Intelligence (AI) Group | Department of Industrial Informatics

This GitHub profile contains the official project repositories developed by the Artificial Intelligence (AI) Group at the Department of Industrial Informatics of the Silesian University of Technology (Gliwice, Poland).

Our Goal

These repositories provide open access to the research conducted by our group and serve as supplementary documentation for our published academic articles.

This commitment to transparency ensures full results reproducibility and offers deep insights into the methodologies and algorithms developed across our research projects.
